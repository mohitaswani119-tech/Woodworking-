Project 01
📦

Floating Wall Shelf

Clean lines, zero visible hardware. Done in an afternoon.

Beginner ⏱ 2–3 hrs 💰 $15 📐 36″×8″
  • 1Cut 1×8 pine to 36″ and sand all faces
  • 2Drill pocket holes along the back edge
  • 3Find studs and mount hidden brackets
  • 4Slide shelf onto brackets and secure
  • 5Stain, paint or oil to your taste
✦ Pro TipA laser level is worth every penny — use it once and you'll never hang anything crooked again.
Project 02
🌿

Cedar Planter Box

Outdoor beauty that genuinely lasts for years.

Beginner ⏱ 3–4 hrs 💰 $22 📐 24″×10″
  • 1Rip cedar fence boards to your desired width
  • 2Cut front, back, sides and base panels
  • 3Drill 4–6 drainage holes in the base
  • 4Assemble with exterior 2″ screws
  • 5Coat with outdoor sealant if desired
✦ Pro TipCedar is naturally rot-resistant — skip the primer and let it weather to a beautiful silver-grey.
Project 03
🪜

Kids Step Stool

A timeless heirloom you build in one sitting.

Beginner ⏱ 4–5 hrs 💰 $28 📐 12″×9″
  • 1Cut two matching side panels on a jigsaw
  • 2Cut the top step and bottom platform
  • 3Sand every edge round — safety first
  • 4Glue and screw the whole assembly
  • 5Non-toxic paint and rubber grip pads
✦ Pro TipRound your edges generously with 80-grit. Little hands find every sharp corner.
Project 04
🍽️

Hardwood Serving Tray

A weekend build that genuinely looks like $200.

Beginner ⏱ 2–3 hrs 💰 $18 📐 18″×12″
  • 1Choose a hardwood board and flatten it
  • 2Cut tray base to 18″ × 12″
  • 3Drill 1.5″ holes for the dowel handles
  • 4Glue dowels flush and clamp overnight
  • 53 coats of food-safe mineral oil
✦ Pro TipMineral oil is cheap, food-safe and makes the grain pop like nothing else. 3 coats minimum.
Project 05 — Weekend Showpiece
🪑

Garden Bench

The one your neighbours will ask you about.

Intermediate ⏱ 6–8 hrs 💰 $65 📐 48″×18″
  • 1Cut 4× leg assemblies from 4×4 posts
  • 2Cut 6 seat slats from 2×4 at 48″
  • 3Build two A-frame side supports
  • 4Attach seat slats with 3″ deck screws
  • 5Sand smooth — exterior stain and sealer
✦ Pro TipPre-drill every single hole in pressure-treated lumber. It splits the second you don't.
